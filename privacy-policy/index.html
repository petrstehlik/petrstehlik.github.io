Privacy Policy

Stehlík Dev s.r.o.  ·  Last updated June 2026

Overview

Stehlík Dev s.r.o. ("we", "us", or "our") develops iOS and macOS utility applications distributed through the Apple App Store. This policy explains what information our applications collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed to function without collecting personal information. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or other identifying data.

Some applications may store user preferences or settings locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs (UserDefaults, iCloud CloudKit). This data never leaves your device or iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

Diagnostics and Crash Reports

If you have opted in to share analytics with developers in your iOS or macOS settings, Apple may forward anonymised crash logs and usage statistics to us through App Store Connect. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or analytics libraries. Any in-app purchases are processed exclusively by Apple; we do not handle or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our applications after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please use the contact form on our website.
